Hello, I am Julia Ou! I am from Livingston, New Jersey and have been doing art since I was around 5 years old. In my recent years, I have tried to express myself more through different types of media. This website was created by me to showcase my artworks throughout the years. I have won several awards/honors including having two of my ceramics pieces displayed in the Morris Museum for the Fresh Perspectives Exhibit and a Scholastic Honorable Mention.
